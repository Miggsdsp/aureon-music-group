# Aureon Music Group — Phase 2 Brand System

## Brand Position
Aureon Music Group is a premium independent music label creating emotionally powerful, commercially focused music across deep house, western pop/country, reggae, blues and Afro house.

## Tagline
**Creating Tomorrow's Classics**

## Brand Promise
Every Aureon release should feel crafted, cinematic, memorable and emotionally connected.

## Colour Palette
- Aureon Black: `#050505`
- Deep Charcoal: `#111111`
- Aureon Gold: `#D4AF37`
- Soft Gold: `#F6DF8A`
- Antique Gold: `#8A6D15`
- Warm White: `#F8F4E3`
- Muted Silver: `#B8B8B8`

## Typography
Use clean, premium modern typography.

Recommended web fonts:
- Headings: `Montserrat`, `Poppins`, or `Playfair Display`
- Body: `Inter`, `Montserrat`, or system sans-serif

## Logo Use
Use the full Aureon wordmark on website headers, presentations and official profiles.
Use the circular star mark for favicon, social media profile image, app icon and watermark.

## Voice and Tone
- Premium
- Confident
- Emotional
- Professional
- Future-facing

Avoid sounding cheap, gimmicky, desperate, or overly technical.

## Core Message
Aureon creates music with identity, emotion and long-term commercial value.

## Artist Roster
- Solara — Deep House
- Ryder Blackwood — Western Pop / Country
- Ganja Boy — Reggae
- Ash Cadwell — Blues
- Zenara — Afro House

## Visual Direction
No visible artist faces in the core Aureon brand until each artist has a complete visual identity.
Use cinematic shots of:
- Studio equipment
- Mixing desks
- Synths
- Piano keys
- Microphones
- Vinyl
- Stage lights
- Soundwaves
- Sunset landscapes

## Social Media Bio
Aureon Music Group | Independent music label creating tomorrow's classics across deep house, country, reggae, blues and Afro house.

## Domain Description
AureonMusicGroup.com is the official website of Aureon Music Group, an independent music label creating and releasing original music across deep house, country pop, reggae, blues and Afro house. The website showcases our artists, releases, videos, music downloads, licensing opportunities, merchandise and future fan subscription services.
